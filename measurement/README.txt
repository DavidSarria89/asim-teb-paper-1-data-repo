The folders containes the data from the spectrum and the lightcurves of the recorded for the ASIM TGF TEB 180916  

The file "TGF_TEB_180916_calibrated_corrected.mat" containes the individual recorded counts (time and energy) in the MATLAB data format.
It can be open with MATLAB or python/scipy

Unit of energy is keV and unit of time is ms, unless otherwise stated inside the file
